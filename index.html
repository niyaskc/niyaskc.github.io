About Us

Classic Health care is a channel partners for MEDTRONIC, PHOENIX, FLEXICARE, MEDCAPTAIN, MEDIANA.Classic healthcare was created in 2008 with the goal of providing cost-effective and innovative medical equipments. The mission of classic health care continues to be to provide solutions that match the best in the world at very affordable prices....

We provide great services and ideas

Classic Health Care is privately owned, leading Medical equipments supplier around northern kerala over 12 years of experience in the health care sector. We provide quality equipments cost effectively, envisioning for a better health care system affordably.

Classic Health Care PVT LTD, is Thrissur-Kerala based company.Deals with five multinational company equipments for various departments.

We are installed the equipments in the top 10 hospitals in private sector in kerala. The doctors, staff, and the patients are very happy to use our equipment.The doctors and hospital staff are very much influenced in our career development.
